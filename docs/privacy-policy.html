Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

TL;DR: FinVantage is designed with privacy at its core. All your financial data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal financial information.

1. Information We Don't Collect

FinVantage is an offline-first application. We do NOT collect:

  • Your transaction history
  • Bank account details
  • Personal financial information
  • SMS or notification content
  • Location data

2. Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using secure SQLite databases. This data never leaves your device and is not synced to any cloud service.

3. Permissions We Request

FinVantage may request the following permissions:

  • Notification Access: To automatically detect transactions from banking apps (optional, privacy-respecting)
  • Camera: To scan receipts (optional)
  • Storage: To import bank statements (optional)

All processing happens locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

FinVantage uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out in your device settings.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability.

5. AI Processing

All AI features (categorization, insights, predictions) run entirely on your device using on-device machine learning models. No data is sent to external servers for processing.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Device-level encryption
  • Local-only storage
  • No cloud synchronization

7. Children's Privacy

FinVantage is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
